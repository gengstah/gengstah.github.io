The four pillars of offensive security.

**4 pages** in this category.

---

- [Exploit Development](/wiki/domains/exploit-development/) — Turning a bug into a reliable, mitigation-aware weapon.
- [Penetration Testing](/wiki/domains/penetration-testing/) — Scoped, time-boxed assessments that find and demonstrate exploitable weaknesses against a defined target.
- [Red Teaming](/wiki/domains/red-teaming/) — Adversary-emulation operations that test detection and response, not vulnerability coverage.
- [Vulnerability Research](/wiki/domains/vulnerability-research/) — Finding unknown bugs in software, hardware, and protocols — and characterizing them well enough to fix or weaponize.
